Scribe Haus: Ultra-Modern Skyscraper Concept

Design Notes by Akosua

Project Overview

Commissioned by Scribe Haus, this 23-story skyscraper is designed to redefine the skyline. The brief was clear: ultra-modern, futuristic, and unlike anything currently standing in Ghana. It had to be bold, functional, and emotionally resonant—an architectural statement for a tech-forward future.

Exterior Concept

The building forms a striking “M” silhouette—two vertical towers joined by a diagonal tech spine. Materials are layered glass, steel, and kinetic surfaces. Integrated greenery softens the brutalism, while the dome base anchors the structure with immersive public zones.

Interior Strategy

  • Modular floor plates for co-working, labs, and executive pods
  • Biometric access, ambient lighting, and vertical gardens
  • Suspended walkways and adaptive zones for tech installations

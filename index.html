Ridge Royal, Accra – Unique, Complex, and Utterly Modern

Explore our ultra-modern 23-story vision for Ghana's tech future

Design Highlights

  • Futuristic twisting diagrid exoskeleton and transparent double-skin glass tower.
  • Animated, illuminated skybridge at mid-height for social interaction.
  • Biophilic sky gardens, green podium, and rooftop urban farm.
  • Modular, column-free interior spaces for R&D, collaboration, and immersive digital experiences.
  • Tech-forward lobby: holographic art, ambient LEDs, AI concierge, and immersive digital directory
  • Integration with urban Accra: animated people, tree-lined streets, modern cars, and lush landscaping.
  • Carbon-neutral-ready: BIPV glass, rainwater management, and real-time environmental dashboards.

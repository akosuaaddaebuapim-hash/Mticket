Scribe Haus: 23-Story Skyscraper — Accra

Interactive model, design notes, and walkthrough-ready layout

Design Notes

  • Location: Central Accra, near Independence Avenue
  • Height: 23 stories (~92 meters)
  • Structural system: Reinforced concrete core with steel framing
  • Façade: Glass curtain wall with vertical fins for solar shading
  • Ground floor: Lobby, retail, and public plaza
  • Floors 2–5: Commercial offices
  • Floors 6–20: Residential units (mixed-use)
  • Floors 21–23: Executive suites, rooftop garden, and helipad
